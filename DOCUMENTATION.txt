3. How to Use
------------
Step 1: Sign Up/Login
- Visit the application
- Click "Sign In" button
- Create an account or log in with existing credentials

Step 2: Generate Images
1. Enter Your Prompt:
   - Write a detailed description of the image you want to create
   - Be specific about elements, style, mood, and colors

2. Choose Style:
   - Select from the available artistic styles
   - Each style gives a unique interpretation of your prompt

3. Select Size:
   - Choose the appropriate dimension for your needs
   - Consider where you'll use the image (social media, print, etc.)

4. Generate:
   - Click the "Generate Image" button
   - Wait for the AI to create your image (typically takes 10-20 seconds)

Step 3: View & Manage Images
- All generated images appear in "Your Creations" section
- Images are automatically saved to your gallery
- View full details including prompt and style used

5. Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions:

Login Issues:

- Check device compatibility
- Adjust screen resolution
